Most tutor searches start with "Year 8 maths" or "HSC English." Necessary. Not sufficient.

Two tutors can both know the syllabus. One gets your child talking. One triggers silence.

## Fit includes

- **Level** — work in the ZPD, not too easy or hard
- **Pace** — moves on when they're ready, not when the clock says
- **Tone** — firm without shame; children who've shut down need that calibrated
- **School alignment** — [still in classrooms](/blog/classroom-active-tutors) beats generic expertise

## How to test fit cheaply

One session. Watch whether your child engages, not just whether answers were correct.
