Parents call us when children struggle. We also hear from parents whose children **sail through tutoring** and still underperform when it counts.

Different problem. Same root: **wrong level**.

## Signs

- "This is boring"
- Finished fast, sloppy under exam conditions
- Tutor never stretches them
- Marks don't match what you know they can do on harder material

## What helps

Exam-style application. Harder problems in the same topic. Talking through _how_ they knew an answer — not just whether it was right.

## The waste

Paying for ten sessions of content they already own. No stretch, no growth, your child learns tutoring is pointless.
