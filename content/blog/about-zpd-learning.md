ZPD Learning is a tutoring network built around one idea: children learn best on work they _almost_ get — with the right help, then less help over time.

We match families with **teachers still working in schools**, plan terms properly, and start with a [$110 Diagnostic](/enrol?plan=trial) so you're not committing blind.

## What we handle

- Parent intake and context
- Session planning and sequences
- Enrolment, payments, term structure

## What tutors handle

- Teaching in the room or online
- Relationship with your child
- Professional judgement about level and pace

## What we're not

- Pay-as-you-go homework help
- A Gumtree-style tutor directory
- Promises about ATAR miracles

Online or in-home across Australia. Questions? [Contact us](/contact) or [enrol](/enrol).
