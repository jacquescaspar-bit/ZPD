Pay-as-you-go tutoring is seductive. No commitment. Book when crisis hits.

In practice it often means:

- Week 1: tomorrow's test
- Week 2: different subject crisis
- Week 3: tutor re-explains the same gap because there was no thread

The child learns that learning is **reactive**. So does the parent.

## What a term buys you

Time to:

1. **Diagnose** — what's actually wrong, not what's loudest tonight
2. **Sequence** — build skill B on top of skill A
3. **Fade** — tutor steps back as independence grows
4. **Loop parents in** — practice between sessions that matches the plan

That's not lock-in for profit. It's runway.
