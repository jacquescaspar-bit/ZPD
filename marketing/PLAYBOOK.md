~2 hours/week from you, then ~30 min/week ongoing. Don't start Phase 2 until the gate at the bottom.

## After first happy Diagnostic

**Testimonial ask** (email):

> Hi [Name],
>
> How did [child]'s Diagnostic go? If you're willing to share one sentence for our site (first name only), just reply. If something didn't land, tell us that too.
>
> — [Your name], ZPD Learning

**Google review** (only if they're happy): link from GBP dashboard.

---

## Tutor pilot (Weeks 2–4, parallel)

1. Read `tutor-network/tutor-virality.md`
2. Onboard 1 tutor through full cycle
3. After a good term start: "Who else teaches like you who'd want the admin handled?"
4. Track tutor-attributed families in spreadsheet

Don't post "hiring tutors" in parent groups.

---

## Friday metrics (10 min)

Admin: enrolments, funnel stuck at payment, referrals used. Spreadsheet: outreach sent/replies, FB posts, tutor referrals.

---

## Phase 1 gate (end of Week 4)

**Go to Phase 2 if any:**

- ≥3 paid Diagnostics or term enrolments from marketing
- ≥10 funnel sessions with emails captured
- ≥2 positive outreach/partner replies

**If not:** repeat Week 3–4. Don't build Phase 2 tooling yet.
