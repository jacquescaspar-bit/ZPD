# Tutor Contractor Rate Card

> **INTERNAL — not for publication on zpdlearning.com**
>
> Parents see plan prices on `/enrol`. Publishing tutor pay on the public site lets them back-calculate margin. Share rates only in private tutor outreach (one-pager, coffee call, contractor agreement).

---

## Per-session contractor pay

| Plan type               | Parent revenue / session | Tutor pay / session | Gross margin / session |
| ----------------------- | ------------------------ | ------------------- | ---------------------- |
| Diagnostic Discovery    | $110                     | **$68**             | ~$42                   |
| Essential (10 sessions) | $95                      | **$60**             | ~$35                   |
| Intensive (20 sessions) | $85                      | **$60**             | ~$25                   |
| Online (10 sessions)    | $75                      | **$55**             | ~$20                   |

GST included in parent pricing. Tutor pay is ex-GST contractor rate (tutor invoices with ABN).

---

## What a session includes (tutor time)

~1 hour total:

- ~40 min with the child
- ~10 min parent debrief
- Commute buffer (in-home)

**Not paid separately:** session planning, intake review, admin — handled by ZPD (founder + systems).

---

## Payment rhythm

- Invoice per completed session or fortnightly batch (define in contractor agreement)
- Pay after session marked complete + parent payment cleared
- Online plan: lower tutor rate reflects thinner margin — do not offer $60 on Online without repricing parent side
