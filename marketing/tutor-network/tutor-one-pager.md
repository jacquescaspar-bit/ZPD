## What ZPD is

ZPD Learning is a decentralised tutoring network built around the **Zone of Proximal Development** — structured, term-based support delivered by **classroom-active teachers**.

We are not a marketplace that drops a parent in your inbox and wishes you luck.

**Learn more (public):** https://zpdlearning.com/tutors#join

---

## What we take off your plate

| You focus on                | We handle                               |
| --------------------------- | --------------------------------------- |
| Teaching in the ZPD         | Parent intake and insights              |
| Relationship with the child | Learning sequences and session planning |
| Professional judgement      | Enrolment, term billing, payments       |
| Showing up prepared         | Contractor admin and payment runs       |

You teach. We run the plan, the parents, and the business side.

---

## How it works

1. **Families enrol** — often starting with a $110 Diagnostic Discovery
2. **Insights funnel** — parent context, school/year, goals (before you meet the child)
3. **Plan built** — learning sequence for the term; you refine, you deliver
4. **Term sessions** — online or in-home near schools you already know
5. **You invoice as contractor** — clear rates, agreed schedule

## Why tutors join

- **Curriculum-native** — you're already in classrooms; no pretending syllabus is static
- **Planned work** — not improvised homework support every week
- **Term momentum** — families commit to a sequence, not one-off chaos
- **Referral upside** — refer families or colleagues you trust; we track and pay bounties (terms in your contractor agreement)
- **Decentralised** — work near schools you already casual at, or online nationally

---

## Who we're looking for

- Actively working in schools (casual or part-time classroom teachers)
- Current WWCC
- At least 2 years tutoring or equivalent support experience
- Can explain _why_ a task is in or out of a child's ZPD — not just answer keys
