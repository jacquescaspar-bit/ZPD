## What this is

ZPD Learning is a tutoring network built around structured term plans and the Zone of Proximal Development. We're not a marketplace that drops a parent in your inbox and disappears.

**Public page:** https://zpdlearning.com/tutors#join

---

## You teach. We handle the rest.

| You                                   | Us                             |
| ------------------------------------- | ------------------------------ |
| Teaching, relationship with the child | Parent intake and insights     |
| Professional judgement in the ZPD     | Session planning and sequences |
| Showing up prepared                   | Enrolment, billing, payments   |
|                                       | Contractor admin and pay runs  |

---

## How a family moves through

1. Enrol — often starting with a $110 Diagnostic
2. Parent completes insights (context before you meet the child)
3. Term plan built — you refine it, you deliver
4. Sessions online or in-home
5. You invoice as contractor on agreed rates
